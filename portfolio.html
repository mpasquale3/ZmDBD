The Coastal Retreat Kitchen

Kitchen Renovation • 2024 • Room Refresh

When this family reached out, they dreamed of bringing their favorite beach vacation vibes into their everyday life. We transformed this dated kitchen into a bright, airy space with coastal touches. The light blue cabinets, natural wood accents, and marble countertops now create the perfect backdrop for family gatherings and morning coffee.

Modern Farmhouse Bath

Bathroom • 2024 • Design Blueprint

Starting with just a simple refresh request, we discovered the hidden potential in this small bathroom. By removing the bulky vanity and installing a floating sink, we created the illusion of more space. The black and white tile pattern adds personality while the brass fixtures bring warmth and luxury.

The Dreamy Nursery

Bedroom • 2023 • Your Design Blueprint

When this family reached out, they knew they wanted something special for their first baby. We created a whimsical yet sophisticated nursery that will grow with their child. Soft pastels, natural textures, and playful artwork create a space that's both calming and inspiring.
